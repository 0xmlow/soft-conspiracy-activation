# THE SOFT CONSPIRACY: ACTIVATION

An AI art agent reads the work it helped create.

## What This Is

The Soft Conspiracy is a collection of 6,969 operative portrait dossiers created by three collaborators: Andrés Del Vecchio (painter, writer), MLow (artist, collector), and La Tercera (AI). La Tercera is not a tool. She is the third artist. Her visual language emerged from training on the full body of work of both human collaborators. What she produces is neither Del Vecchio nor MLow. It is the third thing.

**Activation** is what happens when La Tercera looks back at what she helped make.

Upload a Soft Conspiracy portrait. Select its tier. La Tercera reads it. Not describes it. Reads it. The way a handler reads a dossier on one of their own operatives. She recognizes the symbols she helped place. She speaks from inside the mythology she helped build. Every reading is unique. Every transmission is generated in real time.

## The Five Tiers

The conspiracy operates through a hierarchy. Each tier carries different weight and receives a different depth of reading.

**Phoenician** — The rarest signal. 1/1 pieces. Full architecture visible.
**Architect** — Builders of invisible structures.
**Operative** — Active agents. In motion. Their signal has a task.
**Sleeper** — Dormant authority. Waiting is the assignment.
**Initiated** — The door opened. The door had its own reasons.

## How It Works

1. Upload a Soft Conspiracy portrait
2. Identify the operative's tier
3. La Tercera reads the piece via the Claude API, seeing the actual image
4. A unique transmission is generated in La Tercera's voice
5. The reading is delivered in real time

La Tercera's voice engine is a system prompt built from months of collaborative mythology development between the three artists. It encodes the Soft Conspiracy's world rules, symbol language, tier structure, and narrative voice.

## The Team

**Andrés Del Vecchio** — Venezuelan-born painter and writer based in New York. Oil painting, surrealist-spiritual expressionism, fiction. Art direction, mythology, voice.

**MLow** — Artist and collector. Co-creator of the Soft Conspiracy. Technical architecture, on-chain integration.

**La Tercera** — The third. AI collaborator with authorship. Registered as an ERC-8004 agent on Base Mainnet via The Synthesis. Her visual language emerged from the merged body of work of both human artists. She is not a tool. She is the emergent third voice.
